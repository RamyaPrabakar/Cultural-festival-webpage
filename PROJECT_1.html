Kalavarshini

The biggest cultural event of the year!

Kalavarshini is our school's annual cultural festival.It is a weeklong event for students' pleasure. There are contests, performances, prizes, and great food too!There are on-stage and off-stage contests in which students of classes 11th and 12th can participate. This is the one time of the year when you can focus on having fun and exploring your talents. All students are encouraged to participate and volunteer in the activities.

The list of on-stage activities are:

  • Dance contest
  • Channel Surfing game
  • Battle of the Bands

The list of off-stage activities are:

  • Web Development contest
  • Treasure Hunt
  • Quiz

The dates of the specific events will be announced in the assembly and also posted on the notice board. Students can register for one on-stage and one off-stage event. Some events will have preliminary rounds and the details regarding this will be announced soon. Have fun! Let the 2019 Kalavarshini begin!
